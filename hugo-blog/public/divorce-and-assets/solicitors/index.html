Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a solicitor for divorce?

Yes. A solicitor ensures your financial rights, property interests, and custody arrangements are properly protected.

Can divorce be handled without going to court?

Yes, many cases are resolved through mediation or negotiated settlements.

How is property divided in a divorce?

It depends on contributions, needs, and financial circumstances, and is decided through negotiation or court order.

Can hidden assets be discovered?

Yes. Forensic financial analysis can identify undisclosed assets and income sources.
