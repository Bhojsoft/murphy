Wills and Probate

Securing Your Family’s Future with Dignity and Certainty

Drafting a will or managing the estate of a loved one who has passed is never just a legal transaction—it is a deeply personal, emotional responsibility.

When it comes to wills and probate, leaving things to chance can result in devastating financial and familial consequences down the line. You need absolute certainty that your life’s work is protected and your family is provided for.

We ensure your exact wishes are honored while treating you and your loved ones with the utmost dignity and respect.

The Hidden Weight of Estate Planning and Administration

Anxiety surrounding the future is very real.

If something happens to me, will my spouse and children be financially secure?

Will my assets be consumed by taxes?

If my aging parents lose capacity, how do I legally protect them without stripping them of their dignity?

Furthermore, if you have recently lost a loved one, the burden of administering their estate can feel overwhelming. Dealing with banks, the Revenue Commissioners, and the complex legal paperwork required for registering probate should not be on your shoulders while you are grieving.

We step in to absorb this stress, providing mature, empathetic, and authoritative legal support so you can focus on your family.

Our Specialised Wills and Probate Services

Whether your estate is straightforward or involves complex business assets, our dedicated team ensures a seamless process.

As your trusted wills and probate solicitor, our comprehensive services include:

Will Drafting & Estate Planning

Creating robust, legally binding wills that protect your assets, minimize tax liabilities, and ensure your exact wishes are carried out.

Estate Administration

Taking on the full administrative burden of a deceased’s estate, from gathering assets and settling debts to the final distribution to beneficiaries.

Registering Probate

Navigating the High Court Probate Office efficiently to obtain the Grant of Probate or Grant of Letters of Administration.

The Strategic Edge: Future Planning with Maximum Dignity

A truly robust estate plan looks beyond what happens after you pass away; it protects you while you are still here.

This is where Martina Murphy Solicitors provides a profound strategic advantage in Elderly Law and Future Planning.

We don’t just draft your will; we look at the entire picture.

We possess deep-rooted expertise in drafting Enduring Powers of Attorney (EPA) and navigating the Decision Support Service (DSS).

If you or an aging parent ever lose the capacity to make vital financial, business, or healthcare decisions, these legal instruments ensure that a trusted family member can step in.

We handle these highly sensitive matters with the maximum dignity and respect vulnerable clients deserve.

Financial Clarity: Asset Division and The Cost of Probate

When dealing with estates, financial transparency is non-negotiable.

We also understand that executors are often concerned about the financial implications of administering an estate.

We provide upfront, transparent guidance on the cost of probate solicitor services.

There are no hidden fees and no dense legalese—just clear, straightforward financial parameters so you know exactly what to expect from day one.
