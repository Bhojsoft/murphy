## Secure Your Affairs with Legally Watertight POA Paperwork

Life does not stop when you are travelling, recovering from illness, managing business commitments abroad, or otherwise unavailable to handle important matters personally.

A properly drafted General Power of Attorney allows someone you trust to manage specific legal, financial, or property-related matters on your behalf while ensuring your interests remain protected.

At Martina Murphy Solicitors, we prepare clear, legally robust Power of Attorney documents that provide certainty, protect your assets, and ensure your affairs continue without unnecessary disruption.

## The Risks of Informal Arrangements

Many people assume that a spouse, family member, or business partner can simply step in when needed.

Unfortunately, this is not how the law works.

Without a valid General Power of Attorney:

* Banks may refuse access to accounts
* Property transactions can be delayed
* Contracts cannot be signed on your behalf
* Business operations may be interrupted
* Important deadlines may be missed
* Financial decisions may be put on hold

Verbal permission carries no legal authority.

Likewise, generic online templates frequently fail to meet the standards required by financial institutions and other organisations.

A professionally prepared Power of Attorney helps eliminate these risks and ensures your chosen representative can act when required.

## The Strategic Advantage: Planning Beyond Today

A General Power of Attorney is an effective solution for temporary situations.

However, it is important to understand its limitations.

A General Power of Attorney immediately ceases to have legal effect if you lose mental capacity.

This is why many clients also discuss long-term planning options such as:

* Enduring Powers of Attorney (EPA)
* Future care planning
* Decision Support Service (DSS) requirements
* Asset protection strategies

By considering both immediate and future needs, you can create a comprehensive legal framework that protects you and your family in every circumstance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between a General Power of Attorney and an Enduring Power of Attorney?

A General Power of Attorney is used while you retain mental capacity and typically assists during periods of absence or temporary incapacity.

An Enduring Power of Attorney is designed to take effect if you lose mental capacity in the future and is governed by separate legal requirements.

### Can I restrict what my attorney is allowed to do?

Yes.

A Power of Attorney can be drafted with very specific limitations and conditions.

We regularly prepare documents that apply only to a single transaction or a narrowly defined area of responsibility.

### Will banks accept online Power of Attorney templates?

Many institutions have strict requirements regarding Powers of Attorney.

Generic online templates may be rejected if they fail to meet legal or procedural standards.

Professionally drafted documentation significantly reduces the risk of problems.

### When does a General Power of Attorney end?

A General Power of Attorney typically ends when:

* You revoke it
* The specified expiry date is reached
* The authorised task is completed
* You lose mental capacity
* You pass away

We can advise you on the most appropriate structure for your circumstances.
