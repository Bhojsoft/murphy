## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are assets divided during a divorce in Ireland?

There is no automatic formula for dividing assets.

The court considers a range of factors including:

* The financial circumstances of both parties
* Contributions made during the marriage
* Future financial needs
* The welfare of any children involved

The objective is to achieve a fair and equitable outcome.

### What happens to pensions during a divorce?

Pensions may be considered part of the overall financial settlement.

In some cases, a Pension Adjustment Order may be made to allocate a portion of one spouse's pension benefits to the other.

### What if I believe my spouse is hiding assets?

If there are concerns regarding incomplete financial disclosure, we can take steps to investigate and obtain the necessary financial information.

This may include reviewing business interests, investment accounts, property ownership, and other financial assets.

### Do I have to go to court to resolve financial matters?

Not always.

Many financial settlements can be resolved through negotiation, mediation, or solicitor-led discussions without the need for a full court hearing.

Where court proceedings become necessary, we provide strong legal representation throughout the process.
