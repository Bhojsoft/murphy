# Inheritance & Succession Solicitors

## Protecting Your Family's Wealth and Legacy

Managing the transfer of wealth to the next generation is one of the most important legal and financial decisions you will ever make.

Whether you are planning for the future of a family business, preserving a farm for future generations, protecting family property, or administering the estate of a loved one, effective succession planning requires careful legal guidance.

At Martina Murphy Solicitors, we help individuals, families, and business owners create clear, tax-efficient inheritance and succession strategies that protect assets, reduce uncertainty, and preserve family harmony.

## The Challenges of Wealth Transfer

Many clients spend decades building successful businesses, acquiring property, and creating financial security for their families.

Naturally, they have concerns such as:

* Will my children face significant inheritance tax liabilities?
* How can I transfer a business fairly between family members?
* What happens if someone challenges the estate?
* Will the family home have to be sold to meet tax obligations?
* How can I avoid future disputes between beneficiaries?
* What happens if I lose capacity before succession plans are completed?

Without proper planning, even a substantial estate can become the source of unnecessary tax exposure, administrative difficulties, and family conflict.

Our role is to provide clarity, certainty, and practical solutions that protect both your assets and your family's future.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is succession planning?

Succession planning is the process of arranging how assets, businesses, farms, and wealth will transfer to future generations while minimising tax liabilities and avoiding disputes.

### How can inheritance tax be reduced?

Various reliefs and planning strategies may be available depending on the nature of the assets and the relationship between the parties involved.

We provide tailored advice based on each family's circumstances.

### Do I need a succession plan if I already have a will?

A will is an important component of succession planning, but it is only one part of a broader strategy.

Many estates benefit from additional planning involving trusts, tax structures, business succession arrangements, and future capacity protections.

### What happens if someone challenges a will?

Certain individuals may have legal grounds to challenge a will or bring claims against an estate.

Seeking legal advice early can help reduce the risk of future disputes and strengthen the overall estate structure.
